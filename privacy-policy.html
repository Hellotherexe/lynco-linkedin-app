Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Lyn & Co Realty handles information collected through our internal LinkedIn Post Generator tool.

1. Data We Access

This application accesses your LinkedIn profile information (specifically your member ID via the /userinfo endpoint) solely to authenticate and identify the account on whose behalf posts are published. We also process the post content and image you provide through the tool interface.

2. How We Use Your Data

Data accessed through the LinkedIn API is used exclusively to publish posts to your LinkedIn profile or company page as directed by you. We do not analyse, sell, or share this data with any third parties.

3. Data Retention

We do not store LinkedIn profile data. Post content is processed in real time and not retained in any database after publishing.

4. Third-Party Services

This tool uses the LinkedIn API in accordance with LinkedIn's API Terms of Use. No other third-party services receive your LinkedIn data.

5. Your Rights

You may revoke this application's access to your LinkedIn account at any time via LinkedIn Settings → Data Privacy → Permitted Services. To request deletion of any retained data, contact us at the email below.
